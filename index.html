Hey! 👋

A collection of small demos I created over the years as a teacher. They weren’t originally made for the web, so I've been experimenting with new technologies.

It's a work in progress — adapting them into interactive online versions takes time!

Wondering how these are built ? 🛠️

It's C++ compiled to WebAssembly , with VueJS for interfacing, and statically-generated using Astro.

  • Maze Generator
  • Chess
  • Dungeon Generator
  • John Conway's Life
  • Minesweeper
  • TicTacToe Bot (MiniMax)
  • Breadth First Search

Made with ❤️ in Montréal.
